# SM Pro - Agency CRM

SM Pro is an internal agency CRM used by operators to manage multiple client accounts across service verticals. It is not a self-serve SaaS product.

## Service Verticals
- Social Media Management: Instagram/Facebook content sync, analytics, strategy tagging
- SEO Services: website tracking, keyword management, site health
- AI Receptionist: voice AI for inbound calls (healthcare focus)
- Auto-Blog: AI-generated MDX posts committed to client GitHub repos on a schedule
